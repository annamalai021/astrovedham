Marriage Matching: Find Your Perfect Partner

Marriage matching, also known as kundli matching or horoscope compatibility analysis, is a traditional practice in Indian astrology that assesses the compatibility of two individuals for marriage. It is based on the belief that the positions of planets and stars at the time of birth can influence a person's personality, relationships, and future.

How Marriage Matching Works:

Birth Details: The birth details of both individuals, including date, time, and place of birth, are required.

Horoscope Creation: Astrologers create individual horoscopes (kundlis) for each person based on their birth details.

Compatibility Analysis: The horoscopes are then compared using various astrological techniques to assess the compatibility between the two individuals.

Matching Criteria: Several factors are considered in marriage matching, including:

  • Gun Milan: This is one of the most common methods of matching, which involves comparing the individual horoscopes based on nine planetary points called "gunas."
  • Manglik Dosha: This is a potential incompatibility caused by the position of Mars in the birth chart.
  • Nakshatra Compatibility: The compatibility of the birth stars (nakshatras) of the two individuals is also considered.
  • Other Factors: Other factors such as varna, yoni, and rashi compatibility may also be considered.

Matching Score: Based on the analysis, a compatibility score is calculated. A high score generally indicates a favorable match, while a low score may suggest potential challenges in the relationship.
