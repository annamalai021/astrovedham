Marriage Horoscope: Understanding Your Love Life

A marriage horoscope, also known as a kundli matching or compatibility analysis, provides insights into your romantic life and potential partnerships. Based on the principles of Vedic astrology, it analyzes the birth charts of two individuals to assess their compatibility for marriage.

Key Aspects Covered in a Marriage Horoscope:

Compatibility: Assesses the overall compatibility between two individuals based on various astrological factors.

Marital Bliss: Predicts the potential for happiness, harmony, and fulfillment in a marriage.

Challenges and Opportunities: Identifies potential challenges and opportunities within the relationship.

Timing: Suggests auspicious times for marriage based on planetary positions.
